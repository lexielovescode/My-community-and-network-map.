What is my project

When we talk about networks and communities, all we think about is a lot of complicated net formation. After a few days of thinking and designing, I decided to use the star sytem to demonstrate my networks and communities.

From the center to the outside, it demonstrates my growing process from a little girl to a graduate student. The different sizes of the planets in certain level represent the influence of the networks or communities to my life. When you put your mouse on the list in the left side, you can see the details of these networks and communities and how they influence my professional and personal life.
